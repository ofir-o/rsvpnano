RSVP Nano — Pastel theme palette preview

For the Waveshare AMOLED 1.75 (round 466×466). Three new reading palettes, two candidate variants each. The round swatch shows the sample words “This is” rendered in the body-text color on the palette background, with the focus letter (current-letter highlight) in the accent color. Hex, RGB, and the firmware RGB565 value are shown for each chip (with the slightly quantized on-device color it maps to). Pick one variant per palette, or request tweaks — nothing ships until you approve.

WCAG target: body-text contrast ≥ 4.5:1 against the background (all variants here are well above that). The focus-letter contrast shown is vs the background; the focus letter is an accent that sits among the body text, mirroring how the existing Dark/Light themes use a red focus letter. These are AMOLED-bright backgrounds (always-lit), so they cost more power than true-black Dark mode — fine as opt-in themes, not as the default.
